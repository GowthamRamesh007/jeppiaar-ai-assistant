About The College & AI Portal

Blending decades of academic excellence with next-generation artificial intelligence to deliver instant assistance to our community.

Bridging the Information Gap

Jeppiaar Engineering College has always stood at the forefront of engineering education and technological implementation. As a part of our ongoing digital transformation, the **AI Information Assistant** serves as an intelligent, responsive directory.

This mini-project aims to streamline the lookup process for prospective students, current students, parents, and recruiters. Rather than browsing heavy documentation or calling multiple extensions, users can retrieve specific facts about transport routes, dining menus, hostel amenities, course curriculums, and cut-off marks instantly.

"Our commitment is to provide absolute transparency and rapid, modern student services through advanced natural language processing interfaces."
